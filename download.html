Installation Instructions

  1. Open VSCode on your computer
  2. Go to the Extensions view by clicking on the square icon on the sidebar
  3. Click on the "..." menu in the top-right of the Extensions view
  4. Select "Install from VSIX..."
  5. Navigate to the downloaded file and select it
  6. Restart VSCode when prompted
